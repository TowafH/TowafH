Scuba Heritage was inspired by one of my previous builds on Code.org, which involved clicking items to save the Ocean!

During the 2nd Week at AllStarCode's 2024 SI, I built a interactive version of Scuba Heritage as a group project using the p5 library.

As a group, we created a wireframe to outline the design of the game. Once completed, we presented our game to peers on Zoom
